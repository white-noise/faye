I wish I could compose a message of hate, in a way.
Or a message of understanding.
I don’t know.
A message that allowed my self loathing to become a separate entity, outside of myself, which we could both criticize: pick apart like a body left to rot. Make fun of. Degrade to nothing. Rub down like a stone.
Separation is not so easy.
I realize I am still holding the candle. It doesn’t seem to drip. It burns, but less animatedly than before. Like it is just a candle.
It throws light onto the walls, projecting distended shapes that wobble, growing larger and smaller irrespective of the objects' true sizes. It makes illusions, this candle.
And I am tired of chasing shapes.
I blow out the candle.
And I start to write my email to you.
