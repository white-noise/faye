I start thinking about my email again. I could ask my friends for help. I have a lot of them now.
You would be proud.
It’s not hard to do. You log into a chat room and say things that you like. Share things about yourself. Everyone on the internet is interested in so many things. They have to put it all down into words. Once I talked with someone who told me that they lived in the south, and that even in the fall they have mangoes and pineapples growing where they are. It is fall now, and I cannot imagine those fruit growing here. He asked what we had and I said persimmons. He didn’t seem excited about that. I suddenly know where I am walking toward. It is because of a big mural on the side of a laundromat. It's a pretty mural, for a laundromat.
Some of the people I meet online tell me sadder things. Whole stories of their lives, and thing that have happened to people closer to them.
I feel like one big ear.
But someone has to listen to them, and maybe someday I will have something to say.
Like I said, I have a lot of friends online now. And usually at least one of them is happy. I share what I write. I describe streets around here. I describe what I am learning in school, and how it is so different from everything else I see all day.
I don’t talk about you much. But maybe if I did I could figure out this email.
I can take a break or I can continue on.
back to home
